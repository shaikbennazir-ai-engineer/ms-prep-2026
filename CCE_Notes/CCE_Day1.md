Data Communication = 5 parts

OSI Model = 7 layers
7-App : HTTP
6-Presentation : SSL
5-Session : NetBIOS
4-Transport : TCP , UDP
3-Network : IP , Router
2-Data Link : MAC , Switch

TCP  vs  UDP
TCP = Reliable , slow. Ex: Email
UDP = Fast , no guarantee. Ex: Video call
